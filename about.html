The Legacy of Jinwoo's Treehouse

Hidden in the forgotten forest for over a thousand years, Jinwoo's Treehouse stands as a living archive of magical pacts and tiered symbiosis. Here, creatures of unknown origin—known as PETS—forge eternal bonds with chosen Collectors. As the Shadow Monarch, Jinwoo has been chosen as Treehouse Keeper, it here where Collectors can buy, sell, and or negotiate for their perspective PETS.

What Are PETS?

PETS (Predatory Entities in Tiered Symbiosis) evolve through tiers based on their bond with their partner. From Protectors to Dissonants, each transformation reveals deeper mysteries and powers. There are 4 tiers of Symbiosis: Tier 1, Protector. Tier 2, Shield. Tier 3, Champion. Tier 4, Dissonant.

The Scroll's Purpose

Every tale written here is part of a grand lineage — stories passed down from Collector to Collector. Now unsealed, this scroll reveals the true origin of the PETS and the Treehouse that binds them.
